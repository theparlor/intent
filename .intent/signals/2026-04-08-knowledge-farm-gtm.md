## Observation

The Knowledge Engine is a production platform. Its outputs (personas, dossiers, industry scans, domain models) have value as portable artifacts scoped to an engagement or user.

The **Knowledge Farm** is the carry-away product: a curated knowledge base instance containing:
- Personas & persona renderings
- Company dossiers
- Industry scans
- Domain models
- Product taxonomies

## Business Model

The Farm is a one-time deliverable (the snapshot). The Engine provides freshening (ongoing updates). This creates a subscription relationship:
- **Farm:** delivered as part of a consulting engagement or sold as a standalone product
- **Engine access:** subscription for freshening, enrichment, and new artifact generation

## Parallel

This mirrors the Fieldbook model:
- Fieldbook = portable expense lifecycle (carry-away)
- Knowledge Farm = portable knowledge base (carry-away)
- Both require platform access for ongoing value
