# Engineer’s team rewired around AI: tickets became bot specs, refinement became design sessions

Ari’s team organically evolved their workflow when AI agents started doing implementation. Tickets stopped being task descriptions for humans and became specs written for bots. Refinement meetings stopped being estimation sessions and became collaborative design sessions.

This is primary evidence for H1 (the problem is real) and H2 (spec quality is the bottleneck). The team didn’t read a methodology — they arrived at the same conclusions through lived experience.
