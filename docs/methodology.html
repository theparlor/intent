Methodology

The shift from ceremony to loop. What replaces standup, planning, retro, and backlog grooming — and the heuristics that guide every decision.

The Loop

Intent replaces agile ceremonies with a continuous notice-spec-execute-observe loop. Instead of timeboxed rituals, work flows through the system based on dependency graphs and signal strength.

From Tickets to Contracts

Traditional tickets are prose. Specs in Intent are declarative contracts that agents can execute against. Done is verifiable: when assertions pass, not when someone moves a card.

Signals Replace Backlogs

Signals are captured continuously from work, research, and conversation. They flow into intents, which crystallize into specs. No quarterly grooming ceremony — the intake is living.

Observation Replaces Estimation

Don't guess duration. Observe what happened. Traces from execution feed directly into the observe phase, where patterns emerge and the system learns from its own behavior.

Dashboard Over Standup

OpenTelemetry-compatible events make work visible at all times. Status updates are ambient — available on demand, not forced into 15-minute ceremonies.

Continuous Learning Instead of Retros

An agent reads execution traces daily and writes observations. Learning is a system property, not something that happens once a sprint in a meeting room.
