Intent

Operating System for AI-Augmented Teams

Intent is a personal operating system for knowledge workers using AI as a team member. It's built on a notice→spec→execute→observe loop that turns everyday work into structured, observable, reproducible processes.

Core Concepts

Notice

Identify problems, opportunities, or patterns in your work. Turn vague dissatisfaction into concrete observations.

Spec

Write down what you want to build or change. Specifications make intent explicit and give AI systems something to work with.

Execute

Use AI tools to build, automate, or refine. AI agents execute the spec reliably and leave observable traces.

Observe

Watch what happened. Build feedback loops that make your work visible and improvable.

Why Intent Matters

Most AI tools are built for individual tasks—write an email, code a function, answer a question. But teams that win with AI are structuring entire workflows around it. They're turning vague requests into specs, using AI to execute predictably, and building observation systems to close the loop.

Intent is a framework for doing this. It's not about replacing thinking—it's about making thinking visible, executable, and improvable.
