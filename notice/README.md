# Notice

> Signals, observations, and raw inputs that triggered this work.

The Notice phase is about *paying attention*. A notice is anything that creates a gap between the world as it is and the world as it should be: a friction in a workflow, a pattern across conversations, a technology shift that opens a new possibility.

Notices accumulate naturally. The discipline is not to jump straight to solutions. When two or three notices cluster around the same area, a spec is warranted.

---

## What Lives Here

- **Raw conversations** that surfaced the insight
- **Session origins** — how and when this work started
- **External signals** — market observations, competitor moves, practitioner pain points
